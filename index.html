Internship: SDSU Artificial Intelligence Lab

At SDSU's Artificial Intelligence Lab my job focused on a contract with the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) on ways to upgrade national security with modern technology. The scope of the project, was to improve screening at ports of entry docks, airports and extend how data can be used and interpreted. My focus was on using the Hololens 2 to analyze data in an AR environment. As project leader for our DHS project I developed: loading data to UE4, UI interactions that enhanced data analysis which included creating buttons to enhance on a specific data point, resetting previous data, and updating data in real time.
